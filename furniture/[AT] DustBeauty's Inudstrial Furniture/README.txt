INSTALLATION:
╰─▸install the requirements: SMAPI, Alternative Textures 
╰─▸put the mod (and the requirements) in your Stardew Valley/Mods folder
╰─▸load the game

USE:
╰─▸buy the 'paint bucket' tool from Robin's shop
╰─▸for PC users, click on the furniture you want to alternate using your paint bucket tool. Select the texture you like.
╰─▸for android users, using your paint bucket, you have to long press on the furniture you want to alter/change and select 
which texture you'd like to replace it with.
